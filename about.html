Otter Oaks

Get to know each character here at Otter Oaks!

Otette

Bold and enthusiastic, Otette is a tough otter that struggles to figure herself out.

A menace in the kitchen, a brute in social situations, and a complete crybaby.

Ency

Not much is known about Ency.

Though many know him to be mild mannered and a comedian, there is still much to be revealed about this otter.

Slam

A lanky stork that tends to enjoy solitude but enjoys the company of his small group of friends

Slam struggles with his own awkwardness in his journey to be more open with others.

Iz

To say Iz has an electric personality is to put it mildly.

High energy and willing to put himself in any situation to be in the spotlight is what he lives for. He gives his counterpart Mg grief with every abrupt action.

Mg

Mg is the other half of Iz as well as his complete opposite.

A worrywart that's just trying to keep it together long enough to keep Iz alive.

Mitz

Like many cats, Mitz chooses when she wants to pay anyone any mind

Reserved and quiet, Mitz only enjoys the company of her sisters Ann and Otette. Occasionally she'll accept the group to be in her presence, but never for too long.

Ann

Young Ann is a teenage bear that aims to find social media stardom.

Like many internet citizens, Ann found herself unhappy with her irl life. Dealing with self-image issues and bullies of the past, Ann wants to reinvent herself and learn to love herself.

Rac

If he could simply be at shows week after week he would.

His passion for the music he loves is the only thing that get Rac to leave his bed in the morning to go to work. Struggling with past relationship trauma, Rac seeks to figure out himself.

Bram

If he could play games all day he would.

Bram is a socially awkward sheep with little to no confidence. His friends mean well, and he knows it, but he struggles to contect with friends he's know since infancy.

About the Author

Otter Oaks came from the mind of a mid-twenties corporate worker.

All inspired by day to day life and experiences that mirror many, I want to illustrate the lives of those I know and love.

Everyday our stories are being written, so to see something relatable from time to time in the form of cute little animals might make it a bit easier to process.

I've always found that reading the comics with the more poorly drawn characters and wonderully written story are the ones worth reading. As someone whos seen a thing or two I guess this is my way of putting my two cents into the world. I hope my stories and experiences can help people get through their days, process their feelings, and learn from my mistakes. My hope is that people see this and relate to it and feel a contection not only myself, but to the community that could form in sharing our live with each other.

Yaritza Bejarano - Otter Oaks 2022
